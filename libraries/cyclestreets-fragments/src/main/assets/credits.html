Mapping data courtesy of OpenStreetMap. Without OSM mappers on the ground collecting great data, CycleStreets would not be possible. OpenStreetMap data can be used freely under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0 license.

OpenStreetMap is the Wiki of maps. YOU can help improve the routing and location data, by going to www.cyclestreets.net/edit and contributing your local knowledge!

Map tiles thanks to OpenStreetMap, OpenCycleMap and Ordnance Survey Open Data.

Routing by ourselves! - www.cyclestreets.net

Feedback on planned routes is very welcome, at www.cyclestreets.net/feedback or by through the Comment on this route menu item, and helps us improve the algorithms and data.

Android App written by Jez Higgins and Christopher Fraser, Jonathan Gray, Theodore Hong, Shaun McDonald, Simon Nuttall, Colin Watson.

The App uses, with thanks, the following libraries : OSMDroid - the OpenStreetMap tools for Android, MapsForge - offline vector OSM renderer, and ACRA - application crash reporting for Android.

Part of the development of this App has been funded by Cycling Scotland.
