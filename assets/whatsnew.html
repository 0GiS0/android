Release 1.x

  • New Points of Interest main menu option. Show points of interest, including cycle parking, cafes, post boxes, and London Cycle Hire points, on the route map.
  • New main menu option Open route number. Journey number is also shown on the itinerary tab.
  • Saved routes can be renamed. Press and hold on a saved route to bring up the menu, then choose Rename.
  • Itinerary now estimated CO2 saving, and calories used.
